My Favorite Albums

I go to a lot of concerts and I collect vinyl, so I thought I would make a list of some of my favorite albums.

The Who - Quadrophenia

This is definitely my all time favorite. I'm not a huge fan of the movie nor of this band but this album really makes my ears happy.

The Raconteurs - Consolers of the Lonely

I love all of Jack White's bands. He is my favorite artist and is one of the reasons I started collecting vinyl. I own all of his albums and one of them is worth a thousand dollars.

Portugal, the Man - In The Mountain In The Cloud

This band is amazing. I actually just saw them live for the first time a few months ago at The Pageant. They have four albums I particularly enjoy but this is probably my favorite if I had to pick one.

Father John Misty - Fear Fun

I'm super excited I get to see Father John again this Friday at The Peabody Opera House.

The Mars Volta - De-Loused In The Comatorium

I have seen these guys a couple times. The first time I saw them was in April 2008 and it was the best concert I have ever been to. They were touring their album The Bedlam in Goliath and played for 3 hours with no opening band. I remember seeing the drummer lose his stick at one point and pulled another out of his pocket without missing a beat. Insane band I wish they were still together.

Shakey Graves - Roll The Bones

This guy is incredible. He is usually a one man band, though sometimes he has a group. He creates kick drums out of samsonite luggage and plays blues\folk music. I would definitely recommend giving this guy a listen.
